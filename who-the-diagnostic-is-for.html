Qualification

Who should start the AI Finance Diagnostic Pack?

The pack is for Finance leaders who want practical, independent judgement on how to sequence AI-enabled Finance transformation, not a generic technology conversation.

Start the Diagnostic Pack Prepare with the free AI assistant

Start this if you are trying to answer one of these questions

  • Where should AI start in Finance without creating disconnected pilots?
  • Which use cases should be sequenced first, prepared next or deferred?
  • How do we connect AI opportunities to Finance operating model change?
  • How do we manage adoption, control and governance risk?
  • How do we move from experimentation to measurable transformation value?
  • How should Process, Architecture, Data, Technology and Change teams work together?

This is most useful for

  • CFOs and Finance Directors
  • Finance Transformation leaders
  • FP&A, controllership, reporting and Finance Operations leaders
  • Finance Architecture, Data and Technology leaders working with Finance
  • Senior consultants or programme leads supporting AI-enabled Finance transformation

This is probably not for you if

  • You want a free exploratory call.
  • You are looking for generic AI tool recommendations without a Finance transformation problem.
  • You want detailed implementation support before clarifying the roadmap and adoption risk.
  • Your question is unrelated to Finance, AI, operating model, process, data, controls or transformation.

What you receive

You receive free assistant preparation, one expert hour, a live review of your Finance AI challenge, a concise post-session summary by email, suggested next 30-day actions and a role-specific AI Finance Playbook to build trust, lead with confidence and drive ROI. Additional advisory time can be agreed separately if deeper support is useful.

What to do before booking

Use the free AI Finance Playbook assistant to sharpen your question. Do not paste confidential or sensitive information into external AI tools. Keep the prompt at the level of process, decision, operating model, governance and adoption challenge.

Ready to proceed?

If the challenge matters enough to need independent expert judgement, start the pack and arrive with one clear Finance transformation question.

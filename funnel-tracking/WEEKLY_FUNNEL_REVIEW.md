# Weekly Funnel Review

Use this every week to decide whether the automated funnel is producing useful demand, not just activity.

## Metrics To Capture

- Website sessions
- Assistant clicks
- Paid diagnostic clicks
- Paid diagnostic bookings
- Booking revenue
- Top page
- Top article
- LinkedIn posts published
- LinkedIn enquiries received
- High-potential enquiries
- Possible-potential enquiries
- Low/no-potential enquiries

## Questions To Answer

1. Which page or channel created the most diagnostic clicks?
2. Which page or channel created paid bookings?
3. Which content themes generated LinkedIn enquiries?
4. Are people using the free assistant before booking?
5. Are low-potential enquiries being filtered quickly?
6. Which article should be refreshed or expanded?
7. Which CTA location should be strengthened?

## Weekly Decision

Choose one:

- Keep current funnel unchanged
- Strengthen a page CTA
- Add a new article
- Rewrite a LinkedIn reply pattern
- Create a new pre-qualification resource
- Pause a low-performing content theme

## Minimum Success Signal

The funnel is working if it produces at least one of these:

- A paid diagnostic booking
- A high-potential LinkedIn enquiry
- A repeatable article theme that drives assistant or diagnostic clicks
- Evidence that a specific use case is attracting Finance leader interest

## Watch Outs

- Website traffic with no diagnostic clicks
- Assistant clicks with no booking conversion
- LinkedIn engagement from poor-fit audiences
- Content that educates but does not route to a paid next step
- Too many unpaid bespoke replies
